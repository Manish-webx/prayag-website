Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about cardiac care, treatments, and procedures at Prayag Hospital

Common warning signs of a heart attack include chest pain or discomfort, shortness of breath, pain or discomfort in the jaw, neck, back, arm, or shoulder, and feeling nauseous, light-headed, or unusually tired. If you experience these symptoms, call emergency services immediately. Time is critical in treating heart attacks.

Recovery from bypass surgery typically takes 6-12 weeks. Most patients spend 5-7 days in the hospital after surgery. Full recovery includes cardiac rehabilitation, which usually lasts 3 months. During this time, you'll gradually increase your physical activity under medical supervision. Our cardiac team at Prayag Hospital provides comprehensive post-operative care and rehabilitation programs.

Angioplasty is a minimally invasive procedure where a balloon catheter is used to open blocked arteries, often followed by stent placement. Bypass surgery is a more extensive procedure that creates a new route for blood flow around blocked coronary arteries. The choice between procedures depends on the number and location of blockages, overall health, and other factors. Our cardiologists will recommend the best option for your specific condition.

Yes, Prayag Hospital provides round-the-clock emergency cardiac care services. Our cardiac emergency team is available 24/7 to handle heart attacks, severe chest pain, arrhythmias, and other cardiac emergencies. We have state-of-the-art catheterization labs and ICU facilities ready at all times to provide immediate life-saving treatment.

You can reduce your risk of heart disease by adopting a heart-healthy lifestyle:
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Exercise regularly (at least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week)
  • Quit smoking and avoid secondhand smoke
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques
  • Control blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar levels
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Get regular checkups and screenings
Our preventive cardiology program at Prayag Hospital can help you develop a personalized risk reduction plan.
