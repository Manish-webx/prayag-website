A Legacy of Healing and Hope

Established in 1985, Prayag Hospital has emerged as a beacon of hope and healing in the healthcare landscape. With a steadfast commitment to delivering world-class medical care, we have grown from a modest facility to a state-of-the-art multi-specialty hospital, trusted by thousands of patients.

Driven by the philosophy of "Patient First," we combine advanced medical technology with human compassion to provide holistic treatment. Our team of renowned doctors, dedicated nurses, and support staff work round-the-clock to ensure the best possible clinical outcomes.

We are proud to be pioneers in bringing cutting-edge healthcare solutions, including robotic surgery, advanced organ transplants, and precision oncology, accessible to all strata of society.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to the community with compassion and integrity. We aim to continuously improve our clinical standards and patient care through innovation and education.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized healthcare institution known for clinical excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and ethical medical practices. We envision a society where quality healthcare is a right, not a privilege.

Our Core Values

The pillars that uphold our commitment to excellence

Compassion

Our Journey

A timeline of milestones and medical excellence

1985

Inception

Prayag Hospital was founded with a modest 20-bed facility, driven by a vision to serve the community.

1995

Expansion Phase I

Expanded to 100 beds and introduced 24/7 emergency services and comprehensive trauma care.

2005

Multi-Specialty Status

Inaugurated advanced cardiology and modular operation theaters, becoming a full-fledged multi-specialty hub.

2015

Technological Leap

Introduced robotic surgery and advanced MRI diagnostics, setting new benchmarks in precision medicine.

2023

Global Recognition

Achieved NABH accreditation and recognized as a center of excellence for medical tourism.

2025

Future Ready

Launch of the new Cancer Care Wing and AI-driven diagnostic center for personalized patient care.
